What is CpE?

A four-year engineering program that embodies the science and technology of design, development, implementation, maintenance and integration, of software and hardware components in modern computing systems and computer controlled equipment.

OUR SPECIALIZATIONS

Embedded Systems

An embedded system is a specialized computer system designed to perform dedicated functions or tasks. It is typically characterized by their compact size, low power consumption, and integration into a host device, making them well-suited for controlling specific hardware or providing real-time functionality in various applications, such as automotive systems, consumer electronics, industrial machinery, and medical devices.

Software Development

Software development is the process of designing, creating, and maintaining computer programs and applications. It involves tasks such as coding, testing, debugging, and documentation to produce software that meets specific user or business requirements. The goal of software development is to create functional, reliable, and efficient software solutions that address various computing needs and problems.

Computer Networks

A computer network is a interconnected system of computers and devices that communicate with each other to share resources, information, and services. Computer networks are fundamental to modern computing, facilitating internet access, file sharing, and the operation of various online services and applications.
